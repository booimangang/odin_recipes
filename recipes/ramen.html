Ramen Recipe

Description

This comforting chicken ramen noodle soup is served with homemade honey Sriracha crackers for crunch and extra flavor.

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Total Time: 35 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ¾ cup thinly sliced carrot
  • ¾ cup sliced celery
  • ¾ cup chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Add carrot, celery, and onion.
  2. Cook until crisp-tender, about 5 minutes. Add ginger and garlic. Cook 1 minute more.
  3. Add broth, soy sauce, basil, oregano, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until carrots are just tender about 5 minutes.
