Tortellini Soup

Description

This tortellini soup is vegetarian but packs a lot of flavor and you may not even miss the meat. Pair with crusty French bread.

Prep Time: 10 mins Total Time: 10 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, divided

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan until shimmering over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 45 seconds. Add 1/2 of diced tomatoes with liquid.
  4. Season with Italian seasoning, garlic, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  5. Stir to combine.
